Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

This creamy chocolate fudge is a delightful treat that melts in your mouth. Made with rich chocolate and a touch of cream, it’s perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth or impressing guests at a gathering.

Whether you enjoy it as a dessert or a snack, this fudge is sure to become a favorite. It’s easy to make and requires just a few simple ingredients. Serve it chilled or at room temperature for the best experience.

Pair it with a cup of coffee or tea for a delightful afternoon treat, or enjoy it as a sweet ending to your meal. This recipe is perfect for chocolate lovers and can be made in advance for parties or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 100g butter
  • 200g sugar
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Chop the dark chocolate and butter into small pieces.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the chocolate, butter, sugar, and condensed milk.
  3. Heat over low heat, stirring constantly until everything is melted and smooth.
  4. Add the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt, mixing well.
  5. Pour the mixture into a lined baking dish and let it cool at room temperature.
  6. Once set, cut into squares and enjoy your creamy chocolate fudge!
